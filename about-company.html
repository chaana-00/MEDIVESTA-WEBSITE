COMPANY OVERVIEW

Medivesta Laboratories (Pvt) Ltd, established in 2023 as a subsidiary of Dyvesta (Pvt) Ltd, provides high-quality analytical services in the fields of veterinary pharmaceuticals, food, and feed analysis. We specialize in testing Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients, vitamins, amino acids, mycotoxins, and microbiological parameters. Using the latest technologies, we continuously expand our analytical capabilities to meet industry needs. We are committed to delivering the highest level of service to our clients.

We use world-renowned analytical equipment such as NIR, FTIR, and HPLC. Our team conducts regular audits and performance checks to ensure accuracy and reliability of test results for our valued clients.

Our state-of-the-art formulation laboratory supports the development of liquid, oral, powder, and tablet formulations. It also includes a stability chamber to perform chemical stability studies required for drug registration.
